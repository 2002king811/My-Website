Manifesto 2026

My purpose is to create a united, prosperous, and just Uganda for all. Our 2026 manifesto outlines the key pillars and commitments that will guide our leadership and service to the nation. We invite every Ugandan to join us in building a brighter future together.

  • Peace and Security for All

    We are committed to maintaining peace and security in every region of Uganda, ensuring a safe environment for all citizens.

  • Infrastructure Development

    Our plan includes building new roads, bridges, and public facilities to connect communities and boost economic growth.

  • Youth and Women Empowerment

    We will expand access to education, vocational training, and entrepreneurship programs for youth and women.

  • Healthcare for Every Ugandan

    We pledge to improve healthcare facilities, provide affordable medicine, and ensure access to quality care for all.

  • Water & Sanitation

    Our goal is to provide clean water and improved sanitation to every household in Uganda.

  • Digital Transformation

    We will promote digital literacy, expand internet access, and support innovation to prepare Uganda for the future.

  • Environmental Sustainability

    We are dedicated to protecting Uganda's natural resources, promoting green energy, and ensuring a healthy environment for generations to come.
