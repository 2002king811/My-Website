About

My mission is dedicated to peace, unity, and prosperity for all Ugandans. Founded in 1986, we have led the country through decades of progress, stability, and development.

Hon. Norbert Mao, has served Uganda for over 20 years, championing education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. Under her guidance, the party has implemented policies that uplift every Ugandan, regardless of background.

We believe in inclusive governance, transparency, and accountability. Our mission is to build a Uganda where every citizen has the opportunity to thrive.

Core Values

  • Unity and Peace
  • Integrity and Accountability
  • Empowerment of Youth and Women
  • Development for All

Objectives

  • Promote sustainable economic growth and job creation
  • Ensure access to quality education and healthcare for all
  • Empower youth and women to participate in leadership
  • Strengthen national unity and social cohesion
  • Advance infrastructure and technology across Uganda
