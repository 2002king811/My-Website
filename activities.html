Activities

My party is dedicated to making a real difference in the lives of Ugandans. We organize rallies to connect with citizens, lead community outreach and clean-up campaigns, support education through school visits, promote health with free medical camps, and empower youth and women through workshops and seminars. These activities reflect our commitment to unity, progress, and prosperity for all.

Join us as we engage communities, share our vision, and work towards a brighter future for Uganda.
