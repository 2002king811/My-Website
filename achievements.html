Our Achievements

  • Healthcare: Commissioned the New Mulago Specialised Women and National Hospital, improving maternal and child health.
  • Water & Sanitation: Launched the Nakivubo Waste Water Treatment Plant, providing clean water to thousands.
  • Infrastructure: Built over 2,000 km of new roads and bridges, connecting rural communities to markets.
  • Education: Expanded access to free primary and secondary education.
  • Economic Empowerment: Supported Emyooga SACCOs and other community-based economic programs.
